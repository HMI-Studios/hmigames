Hex Sweeper

⛶ Fullscreen

The classic Minesweeper, but now on a hexagonal grid.

Instructions

Reveal hexes without hitting any mines. Numbers show how many mines are hidden in the six neighboring hexes. Use flags to mark suspected mines and clear every safe hex to win. Once you've flagged all mines around a numbered hex, click it again to chord and reveal all remaining neighboring hexes at once.
